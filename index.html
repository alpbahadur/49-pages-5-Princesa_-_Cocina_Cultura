Our Story

Nestled along the historic Paseo de la Princesa in Old San Juan, Princesa - Cocina Cultura is more than a restaurant—it's a journey through time.

We are the only restaurant in Puerto Rico dedicated to reviving and presenting authentic recipes from 19th-century Puerto Rican cookbooks (1859-1950). Each dish tells a story of our island's rich culinary heritage, prepared with techniques and flavors that have been carefully preserved through generations.

Our enchanting garden setting, embraced by over 20 trees and bonded to a centuries-old wall, creates a magical atmosphere where history comes alive with every bite.

The Rum Library

Discover one of the most extensive collections of Puerto Rican rum in existence. Our carefully curated selection spans centuries of distilling tradition, from rare vintage bottles to contemporary masterpieces.

Each rum tells a story of Puerto Rico's sugar cane heritage, master distillers' artistry, and the evolution of Caribbean spirits. Whether you're a connoisseur or curious beginner, our knowledgeable staff will guide you through an unforgettable tasting experience.

Pair your meal with a perfectly selected rum, or join us for a dedicated tasting session to explore the depth and complexity of Puerto Rican rum-making tradition.

Finca La Zafra

Our commitment to authenticity extends from our kitchen to the soil. At Finca La Zafra, our agroecological farm, we grow many of the ingredients that grace your plate.

Farm-to-Table Philosophy

Our agroecological approach respects the land and honors traditional farming methods used by Puerto Rican farmers for generations.

Fresh herbs, vegetables, and fruits are harvested daily and delivered directly to our kitchen, ensuring peak flavor and nutritional value in every dish.

We also partner with local farmers who share our commitment to sustainable, organic practices, supporting Puerto Rico's agricultural community.
